About Us

Nandan Makkal Dham, founded in 2008 in Belgaum, is a non-governmental organization that runs an orphanage housing around 300 children from various parts of Karnataka, such as Gadag, Bagalkot, Bijapur, Bellary, and Raichur. The children are often found in government hospitals and villages as orphans. Currently, the organization has 25 students under its care. Nandan Makkal Dham receives funds from donors who know them, and they prioritize ensuring that the children are happy, safe, educated, and healthy. The team at Nandan Makkal Dham goes above and beyond to provide a nurturing environment for these children, giving them a chance at a brighter future. Through their tireless efforts, Nandan Makkal Dham is changing the lives of these children and helping to build a better future for them

Vision

Orphan and vulnerable children, especially those infected by HIV, require special care and support to lead a fulfilling life. Providing good facilities and a safe and nurturing environment for growing children can help address their social, educational, and psychological needs. Offering vocational training can equip them with necessary skills to earn a livelihood and become self-sufficient. It is also essential to develop strong linkages between NGOs and government agencies to ensure a coordinated effort towards providing comprehensive care and support to these children. With the right support and resources, these children can overcome their challenges and lead a healthy and productive life. It is our collective responsibility to ensure that these vulnerable children have access to the care and support they need to thrive.

Mrs. Kasturi Banger is the founder of Nandan Makkal Dham. As a single mom, Mrs. Banger takes the responsibility of all the children under her care and ensures that they are safe, healthy, and educated. Her tireless efforts and dedication have made a significant impact on the lives of these children, giving them a chance at a brighter future
